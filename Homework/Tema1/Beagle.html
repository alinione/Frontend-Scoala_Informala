The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound.
The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare (beagling). Possessing a great
sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is the primary breed used as detection
dogs for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle
is intelligent. It is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
